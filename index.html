ABOUT

Fair Play Academy

Created by LaLiga, the world’s strongest league, Fair Play Academy offers young players across the UAE an immersive, professional football experience, with a superior methodology recognized as the gold standard in training and development globally.

In five short years, Fair Play Academy has changed the face of youth football in the UAE, creating a new generation of world-class players and professional athletes.

Training, at the hands of Fair Play Academy’s finest UEFA Pro-certified coaches, is highly personalized, blending technical and tactical skills with cognitive and conditioning, enabling players to embed learning, master the game and heighten their playing ability.

The Academy also offers a professional development track for top young talent looking to pursue football careers or obtain soccer scholarships to universities in the US.

Talents are discovered through Fair Play Academy exclusive scouting program and developed to Fair Play Academy professional standards at the Fair Play Academy High Performance Centre in the UAE.
